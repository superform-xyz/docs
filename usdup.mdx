description: "The UP token: ownership, upkeep payments, validator bonding, staking, and governance participation in the Superform Protocol."
---

UP is the token representing ownership over Superform; it is used to pay upkeep to run vaults, must be bonded by managers and can be slashed, and staking it yields sUP which confers voting power over SuperVault parameters, SuperAsset weights, and ability to set economic configuration.

The canonical UP token is deployed on Ethereum mainnet at [`0x1d926bbe67425c9f507b9a0e8030eedc7880bf33`](https://etherscan.io/token/0x1d926bbe67425c9f507b9a0e8030eedc7880bf33) with LayerZero OFT's existing on Base deployed at [`0x5b2193fdc451c1f847be09ca9d13a4bf60f8c86b`](https://basescan.org/address/0x5b2193fdc451c1f847be09ca9d13a4bf60f8c86b) and HyperEVM deployed at [`0x642fFC3496AcA19106BAB7A42F1F221a329654fe`](https://hyperevmscan.io/address/0x642fFC3496AcA19106BAB7A42F1F221a329654fe).

UP must be staked on Base, with the sUP vault contract being [`0x2c71f70e2Ec720AE061Ae7E0316fC9654d94f417`](https://basescan.org/address/0x2c71f70e2Ec720AE061Ae7E0316fC9654d94f417).
