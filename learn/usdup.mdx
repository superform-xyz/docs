$UP is the token representing ownership over Superform. Staking it yields sUP, which confers voting power over SuperVault parameters, SuperAsset weights, and economic configuration. In current SuperVault deployments, upkeep tokens are configurable by chain rather than universally fixed to $UP, while later protocol phases introduce validator bonding and slashing utility for $UP.

Contract addresses for $UP and sUP across all supported chains are listed on the [Deployment Addresses](/deployment-addresses) page.

## Phases

| Phase | Milestone | New $UP utility |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 0 | Open Access | Initial set of protocol params |
| **1** | SuperVault launch | Validator-secured PPS and chain-configurable upkeep payments |
| 2 | Validator decentralization | Bond $UP to join validator sets with slashing |
| 3 | SuperAsset launch | Voting on SuperAsset composition |
| 4 | Bundler decentralization | Bundlers may bond $UP in a similar fashion to validators |
