title: "$UP"
description: "The UP token: ownership, upkeep payments, validator bonding, staking, and governance participation in the Superform Protocol."
---

$UP is the token representing ownership over Superform; it is used to pay upkeep to run vaults, must be bonded by managers and can be slashed, and staking it yields sUP which confers voting power over SuperVault parameters, SuperAsset weights, and ability to set economic configuration.

Contract addresses for $UP and sUP across all supported chains are listed on the [Deployment Addresses](/deployment-addresses) page.

## Phases

| Phase | Milestone | New $UP utility |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 0 | Open Access | Initial set of protocol params |
| **1** | SuperVault launch | Strategist upkeep burns; validator‑secured PPS |
| 2 | Validator decentralization | Bond $UP to join sets with slashing |
| 3 | SuperAsset launch | Voting on SuperAsset composition |
| 4 | Bundler decentralization | Bundlers may bond $UP in a similar fashion to validators |
